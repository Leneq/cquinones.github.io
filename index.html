Alex Morgan · Senior Technical Writer

I believe that documentation is a product — one that deserves the same care, strategy, and craft as any feature shipped to users. After eight years working at the intersection of engineering and communication, I've learned that the hardest problem in technical writing isn't accuracy. It's empathy.

"The best documentation anticipates the question before the reader even knows to ask it."

My career has spanned fintech, cloud infrastructure, developer tooling, and SaaS — giving me a rare breadth that lets me adapt quickly to new technical domains. I've embedded with engineering teams, sat in sprint ceremonies, and shipped documentation on the same day as code.

Outside of work, I contribute to the Write the Docs community, speak at regional STC chapters, and occasionally post long-form essays on information design. I'm a strong advocate for plain language and inclusive documentation practices.

I'm currently based in San Francisco and open to remote, hybrid, or on-site engagements.
